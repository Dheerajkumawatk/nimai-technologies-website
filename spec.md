# Specification

## Summary
**Goal:** Build a fully responsive, single-page corporate website for Nimai Technologies Private Limited — an AI EdTech company — with glassmorphism design, corporate blue + deep indigo palette, and all CTAs routed through WhatsApp.

**Planned changes:**
- Sticky glassmorphism header with brand name/logo, nav links (Home, About, Services, Why Choose Us, Contact), and mobile hamburger menu
- Hero section with specified H1/subheading, primary CTA (scroll to Contact), secondary CTA (WhatsApp link), and animated tech-inspired background using the hero background image
- About Us section with H2 "Bridging Traditional Pedagogy with Cutting-Edge AI", descriptive copy, and three highlight cards (Innovation, Security, Institutional Growth)
- Services section with 5 service cards, each with title, description, and a WhatsApp CTA button pre-filling the service name in the message
- "Why Choose Us" section with 5 feature highlight cards (icon + title + description)
- Contact section with a 5-field form (Institution Name, Contact Person, Email, Phone, Message) that constructs and redirects to a WhatsApp URL on submission, plus a direct WhatsApp CTA button
- Footer with company name, Quick Links column, Services Links column, WhatsApp button, copyright, and Privacy Policy / Terms & Conditions links
- Two stub pages: Privacy Policy and Terms & Conditions with placeholder text and home navigation
- Full SEO setup: meta title/description, Open Graph tags, Twitter Card tags, JSON-LD Organization schema, sitemap.xml, robots.txt, and favicon
- Cohesive design system: blue/indigo gradients, glassmorphism cards, smooth scroll, scroll-triggered fade/slide-up animations, hover lift effects, and Inter typography
- WhatsApp number kept as placeholder `91XXXXXXXXXX` throughout for easy replacement

**User-visible outcome:** A polished, production-ready AI EdTech corporate website where visitors can learn about Nimai Technologies, browse services, and initiate contact entirely via WhatsApp — with no backend required.
